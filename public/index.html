Anchor Suite

Prove a document existed at a point in time. Hash it in your browser, timestamp the hash with independent authorities, and verify the receipt forever.

This site is for engineers, compliance teams, and the agents that work for them: anyone who needs durable proof of existence for a file or a data artifact.

The anchoring and verification tools are being assembled now and will appear here when they work end to end.

Post Oak Labs never sees your document, never stores your hash, and is not the timestamp authority. The authorities are named in your receipt.
